Competition Categories

Dealer's Den

Participants will be presented with three Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) from which they must select one to address through an innovative business idea. Working in teams of 2-4, they will develop and pitch their business concepts to a panel of judges, demonstrating their entrepreneurial vision, strategic thinking, and potential for making a positive impact.

Learn More

AdMania

Teams of 2-4 participants will create captivating advertisements to promote a product or service aligned with one of the SDG goals. They will showcase their creativity, marketing acumen, and ability to engage target audiences by producing a promotional video no longer than 1 minute and 30 seconds.

Learn More

Business Buzz

A high-intensity challenge where teams compete in rapid-fire rounds testing their knowledge of business, economics, current affairs, and entrepreneurship. This category demands quick thinking, broad knowledge, and the ability to perform under pressure.

Learn More Learners Guide

Product Reboot

Teams take on the role of product strategists and entrepreneurs, breathing new life into failed or outdated products. With limited resources and a live auction twist, participants must create comprehensive business plans to successfully relaunch their chosen product, demonstrating strategic thinking, innovation, and entrepreneurial flair under pressure.

Learn More

The Vision

This year's theme challenges participants to reimagine the fundamental purpose of business in our modern world. We're moving beyond the traditional profit-first mindset to explore how organizations can create sustainable value that benefits both shareholders and society.
